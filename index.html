How I Work

My PM Philosophy

Three principles that guide every product decision I make.

01
Build first, debate second
A working prototype reveals more in one demo than three weeks of PRD debate. I use vibe coding to get something real in front of stakeholders fast — then iterate on signal, not assumptions.
02
AI is a multiplier, not a feature
The best AI products solve a real problem that AI happens to unlock. I evaluate every AI solution through three lenses: does it save the user time, reduce uncertainty, or surface something they couldn't see before?
03
Revenue is the proof
Strategy without ARR is a hypothesis. I architect AI solutions with pricing and monetization baked in from day one — not bolted on after. Every initiative I lead has a clear path from feature to dollar.

About Me

I'm a Senior Product Manager based in Annandale, VA, specializing in AI/ML product strategy across enterprise SaaS platforms. At Employ Inc., I lead AI product direction across three applicant tracking systems serving 27,800+ customers.

What sets me apart: I don't just manage AI products — I build with AI. I was the first at Employ to adopt vibe coding as a prototyping methodology, trained 15 product and design team members, and independently shipped a full SaaS product in production on my own time.

Before Employ, I spent six years at MicroStrategy progressing from product support through Principal TPM to Product Owner — generating $31M in pipeline, training 500+ consultants worldwide, and owning the HyperIntelligence mobile product.

I'm currently exploring Senior and Principal PM roles at FAANG+ companies focused on AI/ML products, platform strategy, and developer tools.
