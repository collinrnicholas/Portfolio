## My PM philosophy

**1. Build first, debate second.**  
A working prototype reveals more in one demo than three weeks of PRD debate. Vibe coding lets me get something real in front of stakeholders in hours — then iterate on signal, not assumptions.

**2. AI is a multiplier, not a feature.**  
The best AI products solve a real problem that AI happens to unlock. I evaluate every AI solution through three lenses: does it save the user time, reduce uncertainty, or surface something they couldn't see before?

**3. Revenue is the proof.**  
Strategy without ARR is a hypothesis. I architect AI solutions with pricing and monetization baked in from day one — not bolted on after.
